HEEGAN Law is dedicated providing affordable top quality legal services and effective solutions to the Somali community inside and outside the country as well as to foreign entities that are interested in doing business in Somalia. These services and solutions will include multiple areas such as legal advice, alternative dispute resolution, civil litigation, administrative litigation, criminal defense, corporate, infrastructure, taxation, foreign investment, foreign employment, legal drafting, real estate development, and petroleum contracts. The accomplishment of these legal services must be based on the principles inspired from our values and our vision.

The vision of HEEGAN Law is to be brilliant, unique and Somalia law firm which can provide effective legal services to its clients and ensure that all right holders get their rights without paying any bribe or being otherwise corrupted. The rule of law will be then realized by following what the law says and recruiting legal experts for the correct interpretation of any disputed legal provisions.
